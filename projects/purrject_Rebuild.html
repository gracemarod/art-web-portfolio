Purrject Rebuild - A Fortnite Lego Island

Project Description

The aim of this LEGO Island is to develop an exploration and building game that supports cooperative play between neurodivergent and neurotypical players. The narrative is how a lost civilization thrived when different minds collaborated, and collapsed when they stopped listening to each other. Rebuilding the world requires honoring every way of thinking.
