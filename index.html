About Me Get to Know me.
I'm a Technical Artist
Grace M. Rodríguez Gómez is a creator with a passion to combine code with art to craft engaging visual stories, games, and films. She loves exploring mixing digital media with traditional art. She has over four years of experience in software development, and multiple years of drawing 2D and traditional. She is currently doing a Master's in Entertainment Technology at Carnegie Mellon, and is looking for more opportunities as a technical artist in the entetainment industry where she can use her expertise in the ever-evolving landscape of immersive and visual storytelling.
